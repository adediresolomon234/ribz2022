OUR STORY

Rahmat and Ibrahim met through their friend Debby (Ibrahim friends wife and Rahmat's friend/cousin) through a group call in which Debbie and her friend were teasing Rahmat. Debby told Rahmat she had a friend for her from the UK who was coming to Nigeria in December and told Ibrahim that she had found him a wife. During this call, socials were exchanged and thus began their beautiful story.

Messages were sent, and conversations were not as regular as both would have liked as Ibrahim was busy working during the days. Still packing during the evenings for his trip to Nigeria, Rahmat was very understanding and patient with Ibrahim. However, their communication became better once Ibrahim landed in Nigeria, they spoke every day, and that is how Ibrahim realised how caring, thoughtful, and selfless Rahmat was. Ibrahim felt that his days in Nigeria would not have been complete without speaking to Rahmat. Looking forward to meeting the girl who has now captured his attention and hers, they both decided and planned to meet on the beach on the 22nd of December. Meeting for the first time, Ibrahim's family were sceptical of his travel arrangements to Lagos as he has been to Nigeria in a long time, but he preserved and travelled to Lagos from Ibadan to meet his girl. Ibrahim was excited on his way to meet Rahmat and couldn't wait to see Rahmat. On arrival, Ibrahim called Rahmat and asked if she could pick him up from the entrance to the beach as he didn't know the place too well. When Rahmat arrived to pick up Ibrahim, Ibrahim could not believe how beautiful and was mesmerised. Ibrahim wondered how she could be so beautiful inside and out, and they walked by the beach. Shortly after meeting, Ibrahim tried climbing some rocks at the beach, which Rahmat could not stop laughing as it was the most dramatic and funniest thing she had ever seen. Rahmat took out her phone and recorded Ibrahim while still laughing till tears came out, and at that point, Rahmat and Ibrahim knew that they had found their one and only. They later walked further along the beach, and that is when Ibrahim asked Rahmat to be his girlfriend, and to his surprise, she said yes, although sceptical at first. Although they did not spend much time together in the lagos, Ibrahim travelled back to Ibadan and then back to London. However, they maintained their daily communication and thus began their long-distance relationship, and their friends can hardly remember times when they were not on the phone together. Although Ibrahim had plans to go travel back the following year, the pandemic happened, and he couldn't; and not seeing each other for a year and not knowing when they would see each other again, Ibrahim planned with the help of his friends and hers, to propose to Rahmat in February 2021.
